The unique identifier of a {{% gl MeshType %}}. All {{% gls MeshType %}},
including {{% gls EntityType %}}, {{% gls PropertyType %}} etc have
a unique MeshTypeIdentifier, which is considered immutable over the lifetime
of the universe.
